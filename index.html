~ welcome to the joint university accessibility_new career website for graduates ~

Established in June 2019, GradsUNIon aims to help graduates gain a better understanding of the various education and career options available, the different universities’ entry requirements and scholarships granted or selecting a job that's of their interest.

Our mission is to highlight that be it, a university route or heading straight into the working industry, there’s always something for every graduate!
